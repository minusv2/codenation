Mushroom soup

Prep: 5m | Cook: 20m | Ready: 25m | Serves: 2

An easy and super yummy mushroom soup recipe. Double cream, garlic, butter and onions makes a wonderful heartwarming starter. Serve with a warm crusty baguette soaked in butter.

Ingredients

  • 50g butter
  • 1 garlic clove, peeled and chopped
  • 2 small onions, finely chopped
  • 350g mushrooms, chopped
  • 500ml vegetable stock
  • Salt and pepper
  • 75ml double cream
  • Finely chopped parsley
  • Finely chopped mushroom

Ready, Steady, Yum!

  1. Heat the butter in a pan
  2. Fry the onions and garlic for 5 mins until softened
  3. Add the mushrooms and delicately fry for 5 min.
  4. Delicatery stire, start enjoying the aroma
  5. Add the stock stock and season with salt and pepper
  6. Simmer for 10 mins until the mushrooms are soft but not squidgy
  7. Remove from the heat
  8. Leave to cool for 5 mins
  9. Puree the soup and process until smooth
  10. Stir the cream into the pureed soup.
  11. Heat through gently for 2 mins
  12. Adjust the seasoning to taste and serve in warmed bowls
  13. Garnish with parsley and chopped mushroom

For the main we recommened a Truffle Chicken.

For dessert we recommend serving with an Apple Crumble.
