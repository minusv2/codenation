Truffle Chicken & Potato Gratin

Prep: 20m | Cook: 1hr 05m | Ready: 1h 25m | Serves: 2-4

A luxurious creamy chicken, potato gratin, and truffle. A delightful comfort dish to enjoy during the winter season.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken legs with the skin on
  • 1 tbsp salted butter, softened
  • 1 small black truffle, sliced into very thin discs
  • 125ml double cream
  • 125ml chicken stock
  • 1 garlic clove, peeled
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 8 sage leaves
  • 500g potatoes, peeled and finely sliced
  • 15g parmesan cheese, finely grated
  • Watercress salad to serve

Ready, Steady, Yum!

  1. Rub ¼ tbsp butter between the skin and flesh on each chicken leg
  2. Gently place half of the truffle discs on top of the butter
  3. Season the skin and set aside in the fridge
  4. Simmer the double cream and stock together with the garlic cloves, remaining truffle, bay and sage
  5. Set aside to infuse for 30 minutes
  6. Heat the oven to 180C/160C fan/gas 4
  7. Butter the inside of a shallow baking dish
  8. Layer the potatoes, adding the truffle slices from the cream between the layers along and season
  9. Strain over the infused cream and stock mixture
  10. Sprinkle over the parmesan cheese
  11. Scatter over the remaining sage leaves
  12. Place the chicken legs on top, brush the legs with the melted butter
  13. Roast for 1 hour until the potatoes and chicken are golden and cooked
  14. Leave to rest for 10 mins allowing the chicken juices to drip into the potatoes
  15. Serve with a watercress salad

For the starter we recommened serving with a mushroom soup.

For dessert we recommend serving with an Apple Crumble.
