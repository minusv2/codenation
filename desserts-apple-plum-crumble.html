Apple Plum Crumble

Prep: 10m | Cook: 50m | Ready: 60m | Serves: 6

This mouth watering apple and plum crisp is an indulgent sweet treat. A crisp and crunchy topping with fruity goodness beneath.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups fresh plums (pitted and chopped, about 15 plums)
  • 1 ½ cups apples (peeled, cored and chopped, 5 apples)
  • 1 lemon
  • 100g sugar
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 175g plain flour
  • 150g butter
  • 50g oats

Ready, Steady, Yum!

  1. Mix the plums, apples, cinnamon, 50g of sugar and lemon juice
  2. Mix well and set aside
  3. In a bowl add the flour and butter cut into cubes
  4. Use your fingers to rub the flour and butter together until they resemble breadcrumbs
  5. Add the oats, the remaining sugar and lemon zest and mix well
  6. Butter an oven-proof dish, arrange the fruit in a layer, then top with the flour mixture
  7. Bake in the preheated oven at 190 degrees Celsius (375 Fahrenheit) for 45-60 minutes or until the top is golden
